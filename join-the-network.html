Join the OwnerRepMatch Network

OwnerRepMatch is building a curated network of independent professionals who represent homeowners throughout custom home construction, major renovations, and high-end residential projects.

Our Application Process

1

Schedule an Interview

Book a 30-minute video interview at a time that works for you. No lengthy application form required upfront.

2

Tell Us About Your Work

We'll discuss your experience, service area, project types, budget range, services, and the clients you work best with.

3

Verification & Profile

We verify credentials and conduct background screening. Once approved, we create your profile from the interview for your review before publication.

Schedule Your Interview

30-minute video call • No application form • Profile created from conversation

What We Look For

Relevant Experience

A track record of representing homeowners on custom residential projects, whether as an independent consultant or in a previous role.

Owner-Focused Approach

Professionals who genuinely prioritize the homeowner's interests, not their own convenience or contractor relationships.

Verified Credentials

Appropriate licenses, insurance, and professional qualifications for the services you provide and regions you serve.

Clear Communication

Ability to explain complex construction and contract matters in terms homeowners can understand and act on.

Professional References

Past clients or professional contacts who can speak to your work quality and integrity.

Geographic Clarity

A defined service area where you can effectively support homeowners throughout their projects.

After Your Interview

Following your interview, we may request supporting materials to complete the verification process:

  • Professional headshot
  • Company logo (if applicable)
  • License details and verification
  • Certificate of insurance
  • Client references (2-3 contacts)
  • Project photos (with permission)

Once verified, we'll draft your profile from the interview transcript. You'll review and approve everything before publication.
