Owner's Representative

CPA MBA, UCLA 30+ Years Experience

Ron brings over three decades of real estate development expertise to every project. As President of EGC Real Estate Group since 1995, he has been directly responsible for acquiring and developing a multimillion-dollar portfolio of residential and commercial properties throughout Southern California.
