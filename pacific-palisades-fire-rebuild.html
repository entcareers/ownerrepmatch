What We Help With

Fire rebuilds are uniquely complex. Insurance negotiations, new fire codes, architect selection, permit timelines — it's overwhelming. An owner's rep brings order to the chaos.

Insurance Navigation

Help documenting losses, understanding coverage limits, negotiating with adjusters, and maximizing your claim before construction begins.

Architect Selection

Connect with architects experienced in fire-retardant design, LA rebuild codes, and the unique requirements of Pacific Palisades hillside lots.

Permit Coordination

Navigate the LA permitting process, coordinate with city officials, and keep your rebuild timeline on track despite bureaucratic delays.

Contractor Vetting

Identify and vet contractors with proven track records in high-end residential construction — not firms chasing disaster work.

Budget Protection

Monitor costs, review change orders, and ensure your insurance payout and personal funds are spent wisely — not wasted on avoidable overruns.

Construction Oversight

Regular site visits, progress tracking, quality inspections, and accountability — so you know the work is being done right.
