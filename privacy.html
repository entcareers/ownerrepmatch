Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Draft Notice: This privacy policy is an initial draft and should be reviewed by legal counsel before being considered final.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Project inquiry details (location, type, budget, description)
  • Professional profile information (for owner's representatives)
  • Interview recordings and transcripts (for network applicants)
  • Communications you send to us

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Match homeowners with appropriate owner's representatives
  • Create and maintain professional profiles
  • Communicate about our services
  • Improve our website and services

Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With owner's representatives: We share project inquiry information with potential matches for the purpose of evaluating a possible introduction.
  • With service providers: We may share information with third parties who assist us in operating our website and services.
  • As required by law: We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic and improve your experience. We may use services such as Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity for this purpose.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at brad@bradhall.net.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at brad@bradhall.net.
