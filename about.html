About OwnerRepMatch

OwnerRepMatch was created to make it easier for homeowners to identify experienced professionals who can represent their interests throughout complex custom home construction and major renovation projects.

Building or significantly renovating a home is one of the largest investments most people will ever make. The process involves coordinating architects, contractors, engineers, and numerous consultants — all while managing budgets, schedules, contracts, and countless decisions.

An owner's representative acts as your advocate throughout this process. They bring experience, oversight, and accountability to protect your interests when you don't have the time, expertise, or bandwidth to manage every detail yourself.

What We Provide

Clearer access to owner's representatives. Finding qualified professionals who specialize in representing homeowners — rather than contractors or developers — isn't easy. We make it simpler.

Structured professional profiles. Each professional in our network has been interviewed and verified. Their profiles include experience, service areas, project types, and working style.

Transparent service information. We believe homeowners deserve to understand what an owner's representative does, how they work, and what to expect before making a hiring decision.

A more owner-focused search process. We exist to serve homeowners, not to maximize contractor leads. Our network is curated, not crowded.

Our Approach

OwnerRepMatch is intentionally small and carefully curated. We don't accept every professional who applies. Each member of our network has been through our application process, which includes a video interview, credential verification, and background screening.

We're not trying to be the largest directory. We're trying to be the most trustworthy connection between homeowners and the professionals who can help them build with confidence.
