How OwnerRepMatch Works

We connect homeowners with experienced professionals who protect their interests throughout custom home construction and major renovation projects.

For Homeowners

1

Tell Us About Your Project

Share your project location, type, budget, current stage, and the support you need. The more detail, the better we can match you.

2

We Review Your Needs

We identify owner's representatives whose experience, geography, availability, and project background align with your specific requirements.

3

Meet the Right Professional

We introduce you to an appropriate professional so you can decide whether the relationship and approach are right for your project.

For Owner's Representatives

1

Apply to Join

Schedule a 30-minute video interview to discuss your experience, service area, project types, and professional background.

2

Verification Process

We verify credentials, review your background, and confirm your qualifications before accepting you into the network.

3

Profile & Matching

We create your professional profile and connect you with homeowners whose projects align with your expertise and availability.

Important Information

OwnerRepMatch does not employ owner's representatives. We connect homeowners with independent professionals. Once introduced, you work directly with the professional you select.

OwnerRepMatch does not supervise projects. The owner's representative you hire works for you, not for us.

OwnerRepMatch does not guarantee outcomes. We verify credentials and screen professionals, but you are responsible for your own due diligence before entering into any agreement.

Contracts are between you and the professional. OwnerRepMatch facilitates introductions but is not a party to any service agreement.
